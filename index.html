// under the hood
Built for accuracy
Five-step CV pipeline from raw video to juggle count.
STEP 01
Ball detection
HSV masking extracts white regions per frame. Hough circles finds candidates, scored by pixel fill inside the circle.
STEP 02
Trajectory tracking
5-frame moving-average smoothing removes jitter. Vertical velocity computed from smoothed position history.
STEP 03
Contact detection
A juggle fires when the ball reverses from downward to upward motion for ≥3 consecutive frames, debounced at 8 frames.
STEP 04/05
Counting + streaks
Each valid contact increments total_juggles. Gaps of 20+ frames without ball detection reset the streak counter.
